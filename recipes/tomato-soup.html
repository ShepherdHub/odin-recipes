Grandpa's Hot Tomato Soup

Description

Another wet one.

This one is smooth.

Put it in your mouth. (Blow on it first)

Ingredients

  • 2 liters Water
  • 1 Big Ole Pinch of Salt
  • 1 kg Tomatoes
  • 1 Pint Heavy Cream
  • (Optional) Love

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water
  2. Bring the water to a boil
  3. While the water is heating, wash and the tomatoes
  4. Roughly cut the tomatoes into cubes
  5. Put the tomatoes in the boiling water
  6. Add Salt
  7. Wait like 30 minutes
  8. Dump everything into a blender or use an immersion blender. Blend until smooth
  9. Add heavy cream
  10. Finish with Love (if using) and enjoy!
