Grandma's Hot Potato Soup

Description

It's wet.

This one is kinda chunky.

Put it in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 2 liters Water
  • 1 Big Ole Pinch of Salt
  • 1 kg Potatoes
  • (Optional) Love

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water
  2. Bring the water to a boil
  3. While the water is heating, wash and peel the potatoes
  4. Cut the potatoes into cubes
  5. Put the potatoes in the boiling water
  6. Add Salt
  7. Wait like 30 minutes
  8. Finish with Love (if using) and enjoy!
